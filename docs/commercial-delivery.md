# Commercial Delivery Notes

DocPipe is currently best positioned as a service-led local deployment tool, not a fully
self-serve SaaS.

## Suggested Offer

**Knowledge-base preparation package**

- collect and classify customer source documents
- run DocPipe locally or inside a private customer environment
- review conversion report and flagged files
- deliver Markdown, JSON, RAG chunks, review checklist, handoff guide, and ZIP export
- help the customer import a small pilot set into their target knowledge-base tool

## Deliverables

- `conversion_report.md`
- `exports/review_checklist.md`
- `exports/handoff_guide.md`
- `rag_chunks.jsonl`
- selected vendor starter export
- `exports/docpipe_export_pack.zip`
- short handoff call or written import notes

## Discovery Questions

- What knowledge-base system will receive the files?
- Are the documents mostly policies, support FAQs, product manuals, contracts, or mixed?
- Are files allowed to leave the customer environment?
- Which files contain scans, tables, screenshots, or bilingual content?
- Who is responsible for final content review?

## Pricing Direction

Use a service-first price while the product matures:

- small document cleanup pilot: 3000-8000 CNY
- private local deployment and import support: 9800 CNY+
- recurring maintenance and new document batches: 1000-5000 CNY/month

Actual pricing should be adjusted after testing with real customer folders.
