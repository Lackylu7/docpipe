# Remote Work Policy

## Purpose

This policy explains how employees request remote work and how managers review those
requests. It is written as a simple sample document for DocPipe demos.

## Eligibility

Employees may request remote work after completing onboarding and security training.
Managers should consider role requirements, customer commitments, and team coverage.

## Approval Flow

1. Employee submits a request with dates and work location.
2. Manager reviews delivery risks and team coverage.
3. HR records the final approval decision.

## Review Notes

Requests involving customer data should be reviewed with the security team before
approval.
